Your Algorithm (Parity DP)

This visualizer demonstrates your space-optimized O(1) space Ruby solution. By utilizing parity variables (even and odd), it tracks the maximum robbery sums dynamically.

Why it works

At any even index i, even + num represents robbing the current house and adding it to the maximum looted up to house i-2. odd represents the max loot up to house i-1. Selecting the maximum updates our running even-parity state.

Complexity

Time: O(n) — single pass through the house array.

Space: O(1) — only tracking two variables (even, odd).
